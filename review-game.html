ABOUT THE GAME

The Beginning of Horror

Resident Evil, dikenal di Jepang sebagai Biohazard (バイオハザード), adalah franchise media horor survival Jepang yang dibuat oleh Shinji Mikami dan Tokuro Fujiwara, serta dimiliki oleh Capcom.

Franchise ini berfokus pada serangkaian permainan video horor survival dan mencakup film live-action, film animasi, komik, novel, audio drama, dan merchandise. Seri pertama dirilis pada 22 Maret 1996.

DID YOU KNOW?

🎬

Movie Adaptations

Six live-action films starring Milla Jovovich have grossed over $1 billion worldwide, making it the most successful video game movie franchise.

📝

Original Name

In Japan, the series is called "Biohazard." The name was changed for Western markets because "Biohazard" couldn't be trademarked there.

🏆

Guinness Record

Resident Evil holds the Guinness World Record for "Best-selling survival horror game series" with over 150 million copies sold.

🎮

Genre Pioneer

The first Resident Evil is credited with defining and popularizing the survival horror genre in gaming.

🌍

Global Impact

The franchise has expanded beyond games into movies, comics, novels, theme park attractions, and even a Netflix series.

🧬

Multiple Viruses

The series features multiple viruses: T-Virus, G-Virus, T-Veronica, Las Plagas, Uroboros, Mold, and more!
